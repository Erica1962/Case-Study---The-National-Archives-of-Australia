The National Archives of Australia

Responsive Design Website

Project Overview

This case study investigated the usability of the National Archives of Australia website from the perspective of a immigrant searching for the arrival date in Australia. A prototype of the homepage was created to address the issues identified to increase navability and readability.

See Prototype

Project Summary

The National Archives of Australia (NAA) is the home of Australia's documented history of important events. Offering online records searches for a wide variety of catalogues, workshops, exhibitions and events the NAA provides the public a place to learn and remember Australia's past. The intent of this project was to increase visibility of the online records and catalogues offerings of the NAA to heighten awareness of the wealth of knowledge freely searchable for students, researchers and the general public.
